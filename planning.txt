This project, agentic-ai-office-automation, aims to build an adaptive Agentic AI system that reads diverse PDF documents and autonomously routes their contents into the correct office application (e.g., Excel, Google Forms, CRMs). The system is an example of Field Force Automation (FFA) powered by LLM-based agents. Professional software practices, modular architecture, and clear milestones will guide the project through research, development, testing, and deployment. The final presentation is scheduled for May 16.

Professional and standard software development practices will be used during the entire scope of the project.
